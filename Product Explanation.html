Excutive Summary


Our Product, the UPet is a revolutionary product that handles all of the foul and time-consuming work of owning a pet. It's like having a pet without all the downsides. All you have to do to keep the UPet running and taking care of your pets is fill up the uPet with water and food once every week (Depends on how many pets you own). The UPet also comes with a built in pooper scooper so it can clean up after your pets.
